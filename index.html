About

Samantha Sparkles

 

 

I worked in a primary school for 5 years, and whilst I enjoyed helping children learn, the thing that made my heart happiest, was being able to make them laugh, and help them feel valued and special.

So I created Samantha Sparkles , where I do just that, by offering Princess appearances and party packages, overflowing with fun games and activities, music, bubbles and lots of sparkle.
